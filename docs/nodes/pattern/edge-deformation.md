# Edge Deformation

## Summary

Modifies geometry boundaries to create interlocking or organic patterns.

---

## Purpose

Edge Deformation adjusts tile or shape boundaries to enable seamless tiling or stylised pattern behaviour.

---

## Computational Thinking Concepts

- Transformation
- Pattern Formation
- Constraint Satisfaction

---

## Mathematical Principle

Applies parametric displacement along geometry edges.

---

## Inputs

Base geometry.

---

## Outputs

Modified geometry.

---

## Parameters

### Strength

Intensity of deformation.

### Frequency

Number of deformation waves.

---

## Visualisation

Edges warp gradually while maintaining overall structure.

---

## Try Changing...

Increase frequency for more complex interlocking patterns.
