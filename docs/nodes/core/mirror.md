# Mirror

## Summary

Reflects geometry across an axis.

---

## Purpose

The Mirror node introduces symmetry by reflecting geometry across a chosen axis.

Reflection is fundamental to many procedural and geometric pattern systems.

---

## Computational Thinking Concepts

* Symmetry
* Transformation

---

## Mathematical Principle

Reflection maps every point to an equal distance on the opposite side of a chosen axis.

---

## Inputs

Geometry.

---

## Outputs

Mirrored geometry.

---

## Parameters

### Axis

Horizontal, vertical or custom.

---

## Visualisation

Geometry flips across the selected axis.

---

## Try Changing...

Switch between horizontal and vertical reflection.
