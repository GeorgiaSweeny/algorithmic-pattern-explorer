# Rotate

## Summary

Rotates geometry around a chosen point.

---

## Purpose

Rotation changes the orientation of geometry without changing its size or shape. It is commonly used to create rotational symmetry and repeating structures.

---

## Computational Thinking Concepts

* Transformation
* Parameterisation
* Symmetry

---

## Mathematical Principle

Each point is rotated around a pivot using a rotation matrix defined by an angle.

---

## Inputs

Geometry.

---

## Outputs

Rotated geometry.

---

## Parameters

### Angle

Amount of rotation.

### Pivot

Point about which the geometry rotates.

---

## Visualisation

Animate the geometry rotating smoothly around its pivot.

---

## Try Changing...

Increase the rotation angle from 15° to 45° and observe how the final pattern changes.
