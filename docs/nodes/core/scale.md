# Scale

## Summary

Changes the size of geometry.

---

## Purpose

Scaling enlarges or reduces geometry while preserving its proportions.

Scaling is commonly used to control pattern density and visual emphasis.

---

## Computational Thinking Concepts

* Transformation
* Parameterisation

---

## Mathematical Principle

Scaling multiplies coordinates by one or more scaling factors.

---

## Inputs

Geometry.

---

## Outputs

Scaled geometry.

---

## Parameters

### Uniform Scale

Scales equally in all directions.

### Scale X

Horizontal scaling.

### Scale Y

Vertical scaling.

---

## Visualisation

Geometry smoothly grows or shrinks.

---

## Try Changing...
