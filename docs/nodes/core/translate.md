# Translate

## Summary

Moves geometry within the workspace.

---

## Purpose

The Translate node changes the position of geometry without modifying its size, shape or orientation.

Translation allows geometry to be arranged, aligned or repeated while preserving its original form.

---

## Computational Thinking Concepts

* Transformation
* Spatial Reasoning

---

## Mathematical Principle

Translation adds a fixed offset to every coordinate.

---

## Inputs

Geometry.

---

## Outputs

Translated geometry.

---

## Parameters

### X Offset

Horizontal displacement.

### Y Offset

Vertical displacement.

---

## Visualisation

Geometry slides smoothly across the workspace.
