# Translate

## Summary

Moves geometry within the workspace.

---

## Purpose

The Translate node changes the position of geometry without modifying its size, shape or orientation.

Translation allows geometry to be arranged, aligned or repeated while preserving its original form.

---

## Computational Thinking Concepts

* Transformation
* Spatial Reasoning

---

## Mathematical Principle

Translation adds a fixed offset to every coordinate.

---

## Inputs

Geometry.

---

## Outputs

Translated geometry.

---

## Parameters

### X Offset

Horizontal displacement.

### Y Offset

Vertical displacement.

---

## Visualisation

Geometry slides smoothly across the workspace.

---

## Try Changing...

Move geometry in different directions while observing how the pattern develops.

---

## Used By

* Escher Tessellations
* Islamic Geometric Patterns

---

## Related Nodes

* Rotate
* Scale
* Repeat

---

# Scale

## Summary

Changes the size of geometry.

---

## Purpose

Scaling enlarges or reduces geometry while preserving its proportions.

Scaling is commonly used to control pattern density and visual emphasis.

---

## Computational Thinking Concepts

* Transformation
* Parameterisation

---

## Mathematical Principle

Scaling multiplies coordinates by one or more scaling factors.

---

## Inputs

Geometry.

---

## Outputs

Scaled geometry.

---

## Parameters

### Uniform Scale

Scales equally in all directions.

### Scale X

Horizontal scaling.

### Scale Y

Vertical scaling.

---

## Visualisation

Geometry smoothly grows or shrinks.

---

## Try Changing...

Compare uniform scaling with independent horizontal and vertical scaling.

---

## Used By

* Escher Tessellations
* Islamic Geometric Patterns

---

## Related Nodes

* Rotate
* Translate
* Mirror

# Mirror

## Summary

Reflects geometry across an axis.

---

## Purpose

The Mirror node introduces symmetry by reflecting geometry across a chosen axis.

Reflection is fundamental to many procedural and geometric pattern systems.

---

## Computational Thinking Concepts

* Symmetry
* Transformation

---

## Mathematical Principle

Reflection maps every point to an equal distance on the opposite side of a chosen axis.

---

## Inputs

Geometry.

---

## Outputs

Mirrored geometry.

---

## Parameters

### Axis

Horizontal, vertical or custom.

---

## Visualisation

Geometry flips across the selected axis.

---

## Try Changing...

Switch between horizontal and vertical reflection.

---

## Used By

* Escher Tessellations
* Islamic Geometric Patterns

---

* Rotate
* Scale
